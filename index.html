Introduction

Nova came into my life unexpectedly but instantly became family. She’s a small tabby with golden eyes, soft fur, and a heart full of love. This page is dedicated to telling her story—one pawprint at a time.

Personality Traits

Playful & Curious

Nova explores every corner of the house. She’s fascinated by movement, shadows, and insects. If you open a box, she’s already inside it before you blink.

Affectionate & Loyal

She follows me around like a shadow. Whether I’m working or resting, she’s always nearby. Nova enjoys head scratches, belly rubs, and curling up beside me at night.

Independent Yet Social

Though she values her alone time, Nova greets guests politely and loves to be involved in daily activities like laundry folding or meal prep.

Nova’s Favorite Things

Food & Treats

Nova is a picky eater who prefers tuna and chicken flavors. She gets one treat after her morning stretch—and she never forgets.

Games & Toys

Laser pointers, bouncing balls, and feather wands are her all-time favorites. She’ll chase a feather for 10 minutes straight!

Places to Relax

Nova loves the windowsill when the sun is out, the top of the couch when it's raining, and my keyboard when I’m trying to work.

Nova’s Daily Routine

  1. 6:00 AM – Wake me up by tapping my face gently.
  2. 6:30 AM – Breakfast time (wet food with chicken).
  3. 7:00 AM – Window patrol: she watches birds
  4. 10:00 AM – Nap in the laundry basket or under the bed.
  5. 1:00 PM – Play session.
  6. 3:00 PM – Another nap, often on my notes or keyboard.
  7. 6:00 PM – Dinner and grooming.
  8. 9:00 PM – Cuddle time and bedtime near my pillow.

Memorable Moments

The Day I Adopted Her

She was the smallest kitten in the shelter, hiding behind her siblings. When I knelt down, she walked right to me. It was meant to be.

Her First Snow

Nova watched snowflakes from the window with wide eyes. Then she tried to catch them through the glass—hilarious and adorable!

The Time She ‘Helped’ Me Study

During my CPA study sessions, Nova would sit on my open books, as if reminding me to take breaks. She’s the best kind of distraction.
